Questions you might have

What is performance marketing software?

Performance marketing software helps marketers plan, execute, and monitor paid advertising campaigns across multiple platforms. It typically includes features for budget tracking, campaign monitoring, and reporting to help you stay on target.

How is marketingOS different from native ad platform tools?

Native tools like Google Ads or Meta Business Suite are built for their own platforms. marketingOS gives you a unified view across all your channels. Track budgets, monitor pacing, and compare performance from one place instead of switching between tabs all day.

Which ad platforms does marketingOS work with?

marketingOS currently works with Google Ads, Meta Ads (Facebook and Instagram), and LinkedIn Ads. More integrations are being added based on user feedback.

Can marketingOS help prevent budget overspending?

Yes. The Budget Checker monitors your spending and alerts you before campaigns overspend. You set the thresholds. You get notified before problems happen, not after.

How long does setup take?

Most users are up and running in under 15 minutes. Connect your ad accounts via OAuth and marketingOS syncs your data automatically. No technical expertise required.

How does pricing work?

Pay per app, not for features you don't use. Pricing scales fairly as you activate more apps. The more value you get, the lower the cost per app. No forced bundles, no complex tiers. Just simple, transparent pricing.
